About ISM

What is ISM?

Independent Study and Mentorship, or ISM, is a rigorous academic program that allows students to explore a career field of personal interest through research, professional interviews, mentorship, and the creation of an original work and final product.

The program helps students build real-world skills in communication, professionalism, and independent learning while gaining deeper knowledge in their chosen field.
