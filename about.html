About Me

Biography

My name is Geet Parekh, and I am a student in the Independent Study and Mentorship program studying neuroradiology. I chose this field because I am interested in the medical field and fascinated by how imaging can help doctors understand the brain and diagnose patients.

Through my research, interviews, and product development, I have learned more about the role radiology plays in patient care and how important communication is when explaining complex topics to different audiences.

Mission Statement

My mission is to explore neuroradiology in a way that combines medical knowledge with education, helping make complex ideas more understandable and meaningful for others.
