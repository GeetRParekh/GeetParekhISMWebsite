Original Work

Original Work – Story Development for "Take a Peek"

The student created a full storyboard and outline for a children's book about radiology, breaking down complex medical concepts into simple, engaging explanations for young audiences. The process included planning page-by-page content, designing characters, and structuring the educational flow of the book.

Final Product

Final Product – "Take a Peek"

A completed children's book that introduces young readers to radiology and medical imaging. The book explains concepts like X-rays, MRIs, and ultrasounds in a fun, accessible way while guiding readers through different parts of the body.
