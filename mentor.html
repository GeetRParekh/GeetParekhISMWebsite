Mentor Bio

Dr. Madhavi Ampajwala, MD

Dr. Madhavi Ampajwala, MD, is a board-certified physician in family medicine with over 20 years of experience. As a primary care provider, she focuses on building long-term relationships with her patients and guiding them throughout their healthcare journey.

Her work emphasizes preventative care, patient education, and personalized treatment. Through this mentorship, I have gained valuable insight into patient care, communication, and the importance of explaining medical concepts clearly and effectively.
